Five Nights at Freddy's

Five Nights at Freddy's (FNaF) is a popular horror game franchise created by Scott Cawthon. The story revolves around Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a family restaurant where animatronic characters entertain kids during the day. However, at night, these animatronics become hostile and roam freely. Players take on the role of a night security guard who must survive five nights while avoiding the deadly animatronics using limited resources.

Cast & Creative Team

Game Series (2014-Present):

  • Creator: Scott Cawthon
  • Voice Actors: Various (including Kellen Goff, Heather Masters)

2023 Movie Adaptation:

  • Director: Emma Tammi
  • Starring: Josh Hutcherson, Elizabeth Lail, Matthew Lillard

Main Characters

  • Freddy Fazbear: The lead animatronic bear.
  • Bonnie the Bunny: A guitar-playing animatronic.
  • Chica the Chicken: An animatronic holding a cupcake.
  • Foxy the Pirate Fox: A broken-down animatronic in Pirate Cove.
